01.Le challenge: mise en place du Responsive, les media queries.

Utilisation de la flexbox, mise en place de 2 formats responsives :
1.RESPONSIVE MOBILE : "@media all and (max-width: 480px)" pour format responsive max-width 480 px Smartphones(Samsung, Iphone, I pad mais pas I Pad Pro 1024px) 2.RESPONSIVE TABLETTES : "@media (min-width: 481px) and (max-width: 780px)" Format responsive min width 481 px to max width 768px TABLETTE I PAD.
